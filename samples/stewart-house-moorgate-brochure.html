Description

Stewart House is a substantial three-storey, self-contained office building of traditional brick and render construction beneath a pitched tiled roof, comprehensively refurbished to a Grade A specification. The premises offer a combined total of approximately 7,520 sq ft (698.63 sq m) of high-quality office accommodation arranged over ground, first and second floors, with allocated parking to the rear of the property.

The building is situated within Moorgate, one of Rotherham’s most established and sought-after professional office locations, occupied by a long-standing mix of legal, financial and consultancy practices. South Grove sits a short walk from Rotherham town centre and benefits from quick access to Rotherham Central station, the M1 (J33/J34) and Sheffield city centre, with the Meadowhall retail and transport interchange under three miles away.

The accommodation is offered on a floor-by-floor basis or as a whole, providing flexibility for owner-occupiers, regional headquarters and growing professional service firms. New full repairing and insuring leases are available on terms to be agreed.
